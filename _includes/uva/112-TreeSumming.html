Summary

Just parse the S-expression into a tree and find all the sums in the tree.

Optimizations

Actually, we don't have to actually build the tree. What we want is all the sums of root-to-leaf. We can traverse the istream in a depth first fashion and find out all the sums along the way without actually building the tree.

Gotchas

Integers can be negative and there might be spaces between negative sign and the number. So be careful about negative integers like - 1 or -1.
